About Us

Quill Box is a modern social chat app designed for seamless communication. Our mission is to connect people around the world. The creators of Quill Box are part of Ente Thoolika, a literary community that was established in 2016. This community is managed by an eight-member panel dedicated to fostering creativity and collaboration. We welcome everyone to Quill Box with open arms!
